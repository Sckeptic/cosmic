You're probably going to smile after reading this.

Maybe you'll call me.
Maybe you won't.

Knowing you...
you'll probably sit quietly for a minute first.

I made this on one of those nights when I wanted to hear your voice more than anything.

I called.

The phone kept ringing.

Then it stopped.

I stared at the screen for longer than I should have, hoping it would somehow start ringing again.

It didn't.

So I opened my laptop instead.

If I couldn't talk to you,
I could at least build something that reminded me of you.

That's how all of this started.

Every page.
Every little animation.
Every word.

Not because I wanted to make a beautiful website.

Because I missed you.

One day, this countdown will reach zero.

The distance will become another old story we laugh about.

These missed calls won't hurt the same way anymore.
They'll just remind us how badly we wanted to hear each other.

Years from now, if you ever find this website again...

I don't want you to notice the design.
Or the code.
Or how much time it took to build.

I just hope you remember how loved you were.

Because every part of this was made with one thought in mind:

"I wish she were here."

And until that day came...

this was the closest thing I had.
